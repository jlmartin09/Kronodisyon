About Kronodisyon

Kronodisyon's interactive adventure will players facing trials of the past. The story mode game that takes players on a journey through the traditions of the Philippines across generations. With graphics that morph and change based on the era, players will feel like they're living in the past while enjoying the modernity of a cutting-edge gaming experience. Get ready to unravel the mystery of evolution as you play and enjoy the ride!

Enjoy the time travel and graphics changing based on the generation of the Philippines. Learn tradition, rhythm your mind, explore the arcade, and face the challenges. Become a KRONOMANCER.
